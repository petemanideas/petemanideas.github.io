What this site is

CalcCompoundInterest.com is a set of fast, simple calculators and practical articles that help you understand how saving and investing can grow over time. The goal is clarity: plug in your numbers, compare scenarios, and make better long-term decisions.

What you can do here

  • Estimate how regular contributions compound over time.
  • Model retirement targets using different assumptions.
  • Compare ETF-style investing vs a savings account.
  • Explore “what if” examples in the Articles section.

Who makes it

Hi — I’m Peter. I built this site to make the maths behind compounding easy to use and easy to explain. I’m not a financial adviser. Everything here is educational and uses simplified assumptions so you can explore scenarios quickly.
