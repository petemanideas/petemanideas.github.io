This Privacy Policy explains how CalcCompoundInterest.com (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use the site. The calculators run in your browser — we do not ask you to create an account.

Information we collect

We may collect non-personal information about how the site is used, such as pages visited, approximate location (e.g. country), device/browser type, and general usage patterns. This helps us improve the site and keep it running well.

  • Analytics data (e.g. page views, time on page)
  • Cookie/identifier data used for analytics and advertising
  • Contact email if you choose to email us

Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar identifiers to show relevant ads and measure performance. Ads help fund the site.

You can learn about how Google uses information from sites that use its services, and manage ad personalisation, through your Google account settings.

Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device. Some are required for analytics/ads to work properly. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but parts of the site may not function as intended.

How we use information

  • To measure traffic and improve site content
  • To maintain performance and troubleshoot issues
  • To serve and measure advertisements

Data retention

Analytics and advertising data is retained according to the settings of the third-party services we use (e.g. Google Analytics / Google AdSense). If you email us, we keep your email only as long as needed to respond.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share non-personal, aggregated data with service providers (e.g. analytics/advertising partners) as part of operating the site.

Your choices

  • Disable cookies in your browser
  • Use browser privacy controls or ad blockers (may affect site functionality)
  • Manage ad personalisation via your Google account settings

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any updates will appear on this page with a revised effective date.
