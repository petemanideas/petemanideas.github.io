$1000 Invested Every Month for 10 Years

Consistently investing $1,000 every month for 10 years can produce impressive results thanks to compound interest. Regular contributions combined with long-term growth can significantly increase your wealth.

Total Contributions

If you invest $1,000 each month:

  • $12,000 per year
  • $120,000 over 10 years

Example Growth

  • 5% return → approx $155,000
  • 7% return → approx $173,000
  • 10% return → approx $206,000

Why Monthly Investing Works

Investing regularly builds wealth through:

  • Consistency
  • Dollar-cost averaging
  • Compound growth
