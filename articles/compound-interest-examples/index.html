Compound Interest Examples

Compound interest is when you earn interest on both your original investment and the accumulated interest.

Example 1

$1,000 invested at 7% for 30 years becomes approximately $7,612.

Example 2

$5,000 invested at 7% for 30 years becomes approximately $38,000.

Example 3

$500 invested monthly for 25 years can exceed $400,000 depending on returns.
