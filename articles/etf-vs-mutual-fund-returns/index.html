ETF vs Mutual Fund Returns

Both ETFs and mutual funds allow investors to diversify their portfolios.

ETF Advantages

  • Lower fees
  • Trade during market hours
  • Often track indexes

Mutual Fund Advantages

  • Professional management
  • Automatic reinvestment
  • Broad diversification

Over long periods, low-cost ETFs often outperform many actively managed funds because of lower fees.
