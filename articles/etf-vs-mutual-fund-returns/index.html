ETF vs Mutual Fund Returns

Both ETFs and mutual funds allow investors to build diversified portfolios.

ETFs

  • Lower fees
  • Trade on stock exchanges
  • Often track indexes

Mutual Funds

  • Actively managed options
  • Priced once per day
  • May have higher fees

Over long periods, low-cost ETFs often outperform many actively managed funds due to lower fees.
